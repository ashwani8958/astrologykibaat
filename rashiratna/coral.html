Coral (Moonga)

Fuel your courage and vitality — channel the bold energy of Mars with Red Coral.
About

Red Coral, known as Moonga in Hindi and Praval in Sanskrit, is an organic gemstone formed from the hardened skeletons of marine creatures called coral polyps. Scientifically referred to as Corallium rubrum, red coral is found in regions like Japan and the Mediterranean Sea and is valued for its vivid red to pinkish-red color.
In Vedic astrology, red coral is associated with the planet Mars (Mangal). It is believed to harness Mars' dynamic energy, offering strength, courage, and determination. This gemstone is considered highly effective in enhancing physical vitality, mental endurance, and personal drive. Red coral is also known to provide spiritual and emotional protection, making it one of the most powerful stones for action and growth.
Note: Always consult an experienced astrologer before wearing red coral, as Mars’ effect varies with each birth chart.

Who Should Wear

Red Coral is most beneficial for individuals under the strong or challenging influence of Mars in their birth chart. It is typically recommended for:

  • Aries (Mesha) and Scorpio (Vrishchik) Ascendants: Mars rules these signs, and red coral enhances their natural strength, confidence, and leadership qualities.
  • Individuals with a Weak or Afflicted Mars: Those suffering from lack of confidence, energy, irritability, or impulsiveness due to a malefic Mars may benefit from Moonga.
  • Professionals Requiring Strength and Assertiveness: Soldiers, police, surgeons, athletes, and leaders in high-energy or high-pressure jobs.
  • Those Seeking Improved Health: Especially individuals facing fatigue, poor blood circulation, low immunity, or skin-related problems.
  • People Facing Relationship Challenges: Red coral is believed to reignite passion, strengthen emotional bonds, and support harmonious marital life.
How to wear

Follow these traditional Vedic guidelines to maximize the astrological benefits of Red Coral:

  • Metal: Set the stone in gold or copper for best results.
  • Best Time: Wear on a Tuesday morning, ideally during the Shukla Paksha (waxing moon phase).
  • Chant the Mars (Mangal) mantra — "Om Mangalaya Namaha" (ॐ मङ्गलाय नमः) 108 times to energize the gemstone.
Benefits

Red Coral is considered one of the most dynamic and energizing gemstones. Its benefits include:

  • Boosts Confidence and Courage: Empowers the wearer to take bold actions and face life challenges head-on.
  • Enhances Physical Health: Supports healthy blood circulation, strengthens immunity, and promotes stamina.
  • Increases Motivation and Energy: Ideal for overcoming lethargy, procrastination, and lack of direction.
  • Strengthens Relationships: Enhances passion, improves marital harmony, and fosters emotional understanding.
  • Balances Emotions: Helps calm aggression, anxiety, and impulsiveness, promoting inner stability.
  • Astrological Protection: Shields the wearer from negative influences of a weak or afflicted Mars.
  • Promotes Safety and Stability: Believed to protect from mishaps, accidents, and harmful energies.
