Pearl (Moti)

"Find peace in the tides of emotion — soothe your soul with the Moon’s Pearl."
About

Pearl, known as Moti in Hindi, is a unique organic gemstone formed inside mollusks such as oysters and mussels. Unlike mineral stones, pearls are created by living organisms when an irritant like a grain of sand enters the shell and is coated with multiple layers of nacre, producing a glowing, smooth gem. This natural moti is cherished for its elegance, emotional calmness, and astrological significance.
In Vedic astrology, pearls are governed by the Moon (Chandra), which rules the mind and emotions. Wearing a certified natural pearl stone is believed to balance a weak or afflicted Moon, offering emotional peace, clarity, and mental focus. It’s often used to treat anxiety, mood swings, insomnia, and emotional disturbances. Pearl stone price depends on its origin, luster, and quality per carat.

Who should wear
  • People with a Weak or Malefic Moon: To calm mental disturbances, wearing a pearl strengthens emotional resilience and stability.
  • Professionals Under Stress: Those in high-pressure jobs can benefit from the cooling effect of pearls, enhancing decision-making and calmness.
  • Emotionally Sensitive Individuals: Anyone facing frequent mood shifts, overthinking, or anxiety can wear a moti stone for mental peace.
  • Students and Creatives: Pearl enhances concentration, imaginative thinking, and creative flow.
  • Cancer Sign (Karka Rashi): In Vedic astrology, moti is especially beneficial for Cancer natives for emotional strength and harmony.
  • Women Seeking Hormonal Balance: Extra Point — Pearls are said to aid in hormonal stability and support healthy glowing skin.
  • Anyone Wanting Inner Peace and Beauty: Pearls are timeless symbols of grace, appealing to both the heart and the mind.
How to wear
  • Metal: Should be set in silver for full astrological benefits.
  • Time Time: Monday, between 5–7 AM, during Shukla Paksha.
  • Mantra: Chant “Om Som Somaya Namah” (ॐ सों सोमाय नमः) 108 times while wearing.
Benefits
  • Emotional Healing: Brings calm, peace, and stability by pacifying a weak Moon.
  • Reduces Stress & Anxiety: Helps with mental relaxation and fights mood disorders.
  • Improves Intuition: Connects with lunar energy to enhance awareness and insight.
  • Aesthetic & Spiritual Value: Offers timeless charm while elevating spiritual grace.
  • Promotes Positive Aura: Helps repel negativity and attract calming energy.
