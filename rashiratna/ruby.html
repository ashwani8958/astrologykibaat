Ruby (Manik)

Ignite passion and leadership — rise like the Sun with radiant Ruby.
About

Ruby, known as Manik in Hindi and honored as the "King of Gemstones", radiates a vibrant red hue and is associated with the Sun (Surya) in Vedic astrology. This powerful gemstone represents confidence, leadership, and vitality, and is known to boost one’s inner strength and self-worth.
Revered since ancient times, ruby is believed to attract love, success, and wealth, while shielding the wearer from negativity. It is the birthstone for July, making it a special talisman for those born in this month. In crystal therapy, it is believed to enhance blood circulation, boost heart health, and increase energy levels.
Its bold energy resonates with passion, protection, and self-assurance, and is said to invigorate the wearer both physically and emotionally. A high-quality natural Manik can be a life-enhancing stone when worn correctly.

Who Should Wear
  • People with Leo (Simha) zodiac as per Vedic astrology.
  • Those in leadership roles, politics, administration, or management.
  • Individuals experiencing low self-esteem or emotional exhaustion.
  • July-born individuals seeking their astrological birthstone.
  • Creative professionals looking to boost personal power and visibility.

Wearing a ruby gemstone correctly can enhance its beneficial properties and align its energy with the wearer. Here are some detailed guidelines for wearing ruby gemstone:
