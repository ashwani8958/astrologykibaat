Rashi Ratna associated with a person's Rashi in Vedic astrology. Each Rashi is ruled by a planet, and based on that planetary lord, a specific gemstone is recommended to strengthen beneficial effects or reduce negative influences.

    Why Wear Rashi Ratna
  • To make good but weak planets in your birth chart stronger.
  • To reduce the bad effects of planets that are in a wrong position.
  • To help you stay healthy, earn more money, feel peaceful, and grow spiritually.
  • It is a type of astrological solution, like chanting mantras, using special symbols (yantras), and doing rituals.

    Gemstones are believed to amplify the energy of their ruling planet through their vibrational frequencies

    How Rashi Ratna Works with Planets
  • Planets emit subtle energy or vibrations.
  • A gemstone of the right type, color, and cut can resonate with that planetary energy.
  • When worn correctly, it acts as an antenna, absorbing and enhancing the planet’s positive influence in your energy field.
